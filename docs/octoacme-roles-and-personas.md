# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers shape the user experience and interface; they ensure solutions are user-centric, accessible, and visually consistent. They translate product requirements into intuitive designs that guide development.

### Responsibilities
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Establish and maintain UI component libraries and design standards
- Conduct usability testing and incorporate user feedback
- Collaborate with Product Managers on feature requirements and user stories
- Provide design assets, specifications, and guidance to Developers throughout the delivery lifecycle

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered designs that meet accessibility and usability standards
- Reduce friction and improve product adoption through clear interfaces
- Align design decisions with business goals and technical constraints

### Typical Communication & Collaboration
- Scoping and review meetings with Product Managers and Developers
- Design critiques and handoff sessions with engineering
- Usability review cycles and demo walkthroughs with stakeholders

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers ensure that project documentation, knowledge bases, and user-facing materials are accurate, comprehensive, and up to date. They translate complex technical concepts into clear, accessible content for diverse audiences.

### Responsibilities
- Author and maintain user guides, API references, and internal process documentation
- Collaborate with Developers and Product Managers to capture feature details and release notes
- Work with QA to validate documentation accuracy against actual product behavior
- Establish and enforce documentation standards and style guides
- Identify and close knowledge gaps across the documentation repository

### Goals
- Ensure all stakeholders have access to clear and current documentation
- Reduce onboarding time by improving discoverability of knowledge assets
- Maintain consistency and quality across all written materials

### Typical Communication & Collaboration
- Documentation review sessions with Developers and QA
- Sprint ceremonies to capture new feature details and acceptance criteria
- Coordination with Product Managers on release notes and user-facing content

---

## DevOps/Platform Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps/Platform Engineers manage CI/CD infrastructure, deployment automation, and system reliability. They partner with development and QA teams to enable smooth, repeatable, and scalable software releases.

### Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain CI/CD pipelines and deployment automation
- Monitor system health, performance, and reliability metrics
- Manage cloud infrastructure, environments, and access controls
- Collaborate with Developers and QA to resolve build, deployment, and environment issues
- Drive improvements to release processes and operational runbooks

### Goals
- Maximize system uptime, reliability, and deployment frequency
- Reduce mean time to recovery (MTTR) for incidents
- Streamline and automate manual operational toil

### Typical Communication & Collaboration
- Release planning and go/no-go reviews with Project Managers and QA
- Incident response coordination with Developers and Support
- Regular infrastructure and capacity reviews with engineering leads

---

## Quality Assurance Analyst

### Role Summary
Quality Assurance Analysts are responsible for comprehensive test planning, execution, and defect reporting. They work in close partnership with Developers and Product Managers to maintain high product quality throughout the delivery lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain test plans, test cases, and test suites
- Execute manual and automated tests, including exploratory testing
- Log, track, and report on defects and testing trends
- Collaborate with Developers to reproduce issues and validate fixes
- Participate in sprint planning to surface testability and quality requirements

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production through thorough testing coverage
- Provide clear, actionable defect reports that accelerate resolution
- Continuously improve testing processes and automation coverage

### Typical Communication & Collaboration
- Sprint planning and backlog grooming with Developers and Product Managers
- Defect triage meetings and status updates
- Release readiness reviews and sign-off coordination with Project Managers

---

## Support Lead/Customer Success

### Role Summary
Support Leads and Customer Success Managers represent the voice of the end user. They manage incoming support requests, identify recurring issues, and relay field insights to Product and Project Managers to drive continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Manage and prioritize incoming support tickets and bug reports from end users
- Escalate critical issues to the appropriate engineering or product teams
- Synthesize user feedback and support trends into actionable insights
- Collaborate with Product Managers to advocate for user needs in roadmap planning
- Maintain customer-facing knowledge base articles and FAQs

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and product adoption
- Reduce ticket volume through proactive documentation and product improvements
- Ensure end-user feedback informs product and process decisions

### Typical Communication & Collaboration
- Weekly syncs with Product and Project Managers to share support trends
- Escalation channels with Developers and QA for critical bugs
- Customer demos and feedback sessions to gather user insights
